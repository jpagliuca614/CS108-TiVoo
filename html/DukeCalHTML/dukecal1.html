Title: "O' Say Can You See" Installation by Laura Poitras<br /> Description: "O' Say Can You See" is an installation by filmmaker Laura Poitras, on view in the Kreps Gallery at the Center for Documentary Studies at Duke University until October 22, 2011. The installation features film footage shot by Poitras in the hours after 9/11 from Ground Zero in 2001, combined with audio from the Yankees' Game 4 World Series victory weeks later, and interviews with recently released detainees from Guantanamo Bay. An artist's talk and reception will take place September 20, 2011, 6-9 p.m., talk at 7 p.m., at the Center for Documentary Studies.
