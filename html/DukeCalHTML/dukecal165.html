Title: Innovation in the Control and Elimination of Neglected Tropical Diseases<br /> Description: The Chancellor's Lecture Series, The Science Advisory Council, and the Duke Scholars in Infectious Diseases Program present "Innovation in the Control and Elimination of Neglected Tropical Diseases" by Peter Hotez, MD, PhD. Dr. Hotez is Professor of Pediatrics and Molecular Virology and Microbiology at Baylor College of Medicine where he is chief of Pediatric Tropical Medicine and founding Dean of the National School of Tropical Medicine. In addition, he is Texas Children's Hospital Endowed Chair of Tropical Pediatrics, and president of the Sabine Vaccine Institute. He will lead the new Sabine vaccine development program at Texas Children's Hospital and Baylor College of Medicine. Reception to follow in the Hall of Science at 5:00pm
